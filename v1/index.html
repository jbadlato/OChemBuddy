Welcome to OChem Buddy!

The molecule namer that will make studying for organic chemistry so much easier.

Directions:

  1. Draw a carbon by clicking anywhere on the canvas
  2. Draw bonds by clicking & dragging between carbons
  3. Click "Name My Molecule" at the bottom!

Only acyclic saturated hydrocarbons are currently supported.


Molecules are named using the 1979 IUPAC recommendations, which can be found in full here
